About LIDE (Link-ID-Entity)

Mission & Purpose

LIDE (Link-ID-Entity) is a full-service digital environment company. We make digital tools accessible, affordable, and valuable for individuals, growing organisations, and enterprises.

Human-First Design

Every solution begins with people — we create experiences that feel natural, intuitive, and impactful, empowering individuals and organizations to grow confidently in the digital world.

Scalability and Ownership

LIDE’s ecosystems expand with your ambitions. We retain ownership of the systems we build, giving clients control and clarity while reducing maintenance burdens.

What We Do

We build and maintain your digital presence across multiple platforms and systems. Our services are grouped into three key areas:

  1. Front Access – Public-facing digital identity (Websites, social media integration, messaging, streaming, community platforms).
  2. Function Access – Tools that save time (Automation, chatbots, workflow systems).
  3. Environment Access – Internal digital ecosystem (Hosting, communication, data management, analytics).

How Our Model Works

  • Setup Fee – Builds your custom digital environment.
  • Subscription Fee – Maintains your access to systems, updates, and support.
